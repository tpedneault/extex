◆ VERTEX_ATTRIB_LOCATION

enum VERTEX_ATTRIB_LOCATION

Defines standard locations for vertex attributes.

This enumeration specifies standard locations for various types of vertex attributes in a shader program. By standardizing the locations of common attributes, it helps ensure consistency across different shaders and simplifies the binding of vertex buffer data to shader inputs.

Enumerator
POSITIONS 

Location 0 is reserved for vertex positions.

NORMALS 

Location 1 is reserved for vertex normals.

TEX_COORDS 

Location 2 is reserved for texture coordinates.
